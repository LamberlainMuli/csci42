Clothing Image Guide

Follow these tips for the best results when uploading or scanning your clothing items:

  • Lighting: Use bright, even, and soft lighting. Avoid direct sunlight or harsh shadows across the item. Natural daylight near a window often works well.
  • Background: Use a plain, solid-colored background that contrasts well with the clothing item. A white or neutral wall/sheet is ideal. Avoid cluttered backgrounds.
  • Layout: Lay the clothing item flat and smooth out any wrinkles. For items like shirts or dresses, consider hanging them on a plain hanger against the contrasting background.
  • Framing: Ensure the entire clothing item is clearly visible within the photo frame. Avoid cutting off parts of the item.
  • Resolution: Use a reasonably high resolution, but extremely large files may take longer to process. Most smartphone photos are sufficient.
  • Focus: Make sure the image is clear and in focus.

Following these guidelines will help our background removal tool work effectively and give you a clean image for your listings or virtual closet.
